We have upgraded more features: Sound Engine, Physics Engine, Particle Engine... * Core functionality: Direct3D11, OpenGL 4+, OpenGLES 3+, Google Angle GLES video driver backends. * Cross-Platform Framework: Android, IOS, Win32/Win64, UWP, Linux, MacOS, HTML5. * Render pipeline: Forwarder & Deferred.
