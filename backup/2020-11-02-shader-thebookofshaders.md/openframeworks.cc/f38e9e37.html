openFrameworks is an open source C++ toolkit for creative coding.

download

Grab the most recent release (0.11.0) and follow the setup guide to get openFrameworks running.

documentation

Reference for openFrameworks classes, functions and addons. You can also check the tutorials section.

forum

The forum is a place to meet other people working with openFrameworks. It's a place to share your work, or find help solving problems.

Donations help support the development of openFrameworks, improve the documentation and pay for third party services needed for the project.

If you are using openFrameworks commercially or would simply like to support openFrameworks development, please consider donating to the project.
